Hi. You're looking at Fleet.

Fleet is a network of parking.
It provides a global and unique access to all parking in the network through plate recognition.
Fleet interacts with each parking in the network, each having its own independent hardware infrastructure,
offering users a common way of access, regardless of each specific parking system.

Pay with your plate

Register your credit card to automatically pay your parking. No more coins needed.

Plate recognition access

Enter and exits from the parking without any issue. Your car plate is your identifier.

Accumulate Fleet point

Gain Cumulus point while your car is parked. Collect your point and gaing Free hours or parking.

Reseve a spot

Be sure to be on time and not waste your time finding a parking. Use Fleet to reserve a spot.

Find the best parking for you

Stop worring about looking for a park. Fleets drives you to the best solution according to your preferences.

Optimise parking resources

Data analysis of the parking usage generates new spots and allows to increas the incomes.

Reduce city traffic

Artificial Intelligence enable to redirect traffic around the city reducing pollution and traffic jam.

Fleet. Just drive.

We offers a unique service enabling drivers to access public and private parking infrastructures by automatically recognizing car plates. One registration using credit card valid for all parking partners, park spot reservation, intrinsically easy to use, less queues, navigation to the ideal parking for a destination.
Our system will maximize the occupancy of the parking and encourage its usage with the intrinsic simplicity.
